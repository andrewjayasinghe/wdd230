Site Name

The site is going to be called "Gales Creek Chamber of Commerce."

Site Purpose

The main purpose of the site is to provide information regarding a chamber of commerce located in the city of Gales Creek in the country of Canada.

The Gales Creek Chamber of Commerce is a local organization of businesses and companies in Gales Creek with the intention to develop and further the interests of local companies and businesses in Canada. Many businesses are international operating companies with offices in Canada.
